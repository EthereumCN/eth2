Eth2 is the next generation of Ethereum. And while Ethereum as you know and love it will continue to exist as its own independent proof-of-work chain for a little while to come, the transition towards PoS starts now.In order to make this transition possible, eth2 requires active participants ‚Äì known as validators. What is eth2 phase 0?

Transitioning from proof-of-work (PoW) to proof-of-stake (PoS), eth2 will be launched in at least 4 phases. The idea is to break up this transition into phases each focusing on a different aspect of eth2.Phase 0 contains all of the machinery behind eth2's PoS consensus, it tracks the validators and their balances.Phase 1 handles adding, storing, and retrieving the data associated with eth2's shards.Phase 1.5 updates Ethereum as we know it today from PoW to PoS by making it a shard under eth2.Phase 2 Phase 2 adds execution to the remaining eth2 shards which enables smart contracts to run on all of the shards.
